How we clean

A simple process that gets the job done right.

The workflow is built around real service jobs: inspect the surface, protect the room, treat the problem areas, then rinse and extract for a cleaner finish.

1

Pre-inspect

Check material, condition, colorfastness, stains, and overall soiling before starting.

2

Prepare the area

Protect surrounding furnishings and floors before the cleaning begins.

3

Pre-vacuum & pre-treat

Remove loose soil, dust mites, and pet dander, then pre-treat stains and heavy soil.

4

Steam rinse & extraction

Loosen, rinse, and extract soil so upholstery and carpet come out fresh and almost dry.
